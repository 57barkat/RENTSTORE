# AnganStay Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

This document mirrors the in-app privacy policy and is intended to be published at a public URL for app-store compliance.

## Information we collect

- Account details such as name, email, phone number, password, role, and verification status.
- Host-submitted identity or business details such as CNIC, agency name, agency license, listing descriptions, pricing, amenities, and uploaded photos.
- Foreground location information used for nearby listings, map placement, and address selection.
- Audio clips when a user chooses voice search.
- Profile images, property photos, reports, favorites, purchase-related activity, and account recovery information.

## How we use information

- Create and protect accounts.
- Verify contact details.
- Publish and manage listings.
- Power maps, nearby results, messaging, favorites, and voice search.
- Process purchases and support requests.

## Sharing and service providers

- Cloudinary for media uploads.
- Google Places for address search.
- Mapbox for maps.
- Backend and payment providers that support authentication, listings, chat, and purchases.

## Device storage

- Session tokens.
- User account state.
- Phone-verification state.
- Theme preference.
- Property-upload queue state.
- Local view-tracking markers.

## Choices

- Users can decline optional permissions such as location, microphone, or media-library access.
- Privacy or deletion requests can be directed to `barkat.khattak@codeshop.biz`.
